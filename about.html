About Kiln Koffee

Kiln Koffee is a small company owned coffee shop. They source all of their produce locally and their coffee beans are ethically sourced from South and Central America, roasted in house. Kiln Koffee strives to be sus- tainable, therefore all to-go cups are biodegradable, and all in-house ceramic mugs and cups are created from the owners in their own studio.
